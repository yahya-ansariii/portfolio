Design and Analysis of Monocoque Chassis

A sketch of the chassis of a vehicle (Mahindra XUV 500) was drawn with the help of actual dimensions from the vehicle. Later with the help of sketch, a 3D model was constructed in CAD software-Solidworks. The CAD design was analysed for stress on Analysis software-Ansys for various impacts such as Front impact, Rear impact, Side impact, and Roll over. In video only analysis is shown.
